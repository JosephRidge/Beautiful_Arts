About Us

This are our core values and mission.

Description: Who We Are

At Lumina Beauty Arts, we believe that beauty is a powerful and personal form of self-expression. We are a collective of dedicated artists and enthusiasts committed to transforming the beauty industry through education, innovation, and artistry. More than just a brand, Lumina is a destination for those who see makeup and beauty rituals as a craft—a way to highlight individual brilliance and build confidence from the inside out. We provide everything from professional masterclasses and bespoke client services to a thoughtfully curated blog, all dedicated to the art and soul of beauty.


Our Vision: The Future We See

Our vision is to cultivate a world where beauty is defined by individuality and empowered self-expression. We strive to be a leading force in shifting industry standards towards inclusivity, sustainability, and artistic integrity. We envision a global community where anyone, regardless of skill level, can access the tools, knowledge, and inspiration to explore their creativity and feel seen, celebrated, and radiantly themselves.


Our Founders: The Heart of Lumina

Lumina Beauty Arts was born from the shared passion of Elena Vance and Marcus Thorne. Elena, a renowned makeup artist with over 15 years of experience in editorial and film, noticed a gap between high-fashion techniques and the everyday consumer. Marcus, a former cosmetic chemist and branding expert, was driven by a mission to create products that perform brilliantly while adhering to ethical standards. Together, they fused artistic vision with conscious formulation, laying the foundation for a company that honors both art and principle.


Our Contributors: The Voices of Lumina

Our platform is brought to life by a diverse family of talented contributors who are experts in their fields. This includes:

  • Lead Educators: Certified makeup artists who develop and teach our curriculum.
  • Guest Artists: Industry professionals who bring fresh perspectives and specialized techniques to our community.
  • Skincare Specialists: Licensed estheticians who provide expert advice on creating a healthy canvas.
  • Wellness Advocates: Writers and coaches who explore the connection between inner well-being and outer glow.

Together, our contributors ensure that the wisdom shared on our platform is not only trend-forward but also technically sound, inclusive, and holistic.
